Sebastian Garcia

QB

Jesus Sebastian Garcia Mendoza (born October 7, 1996) in a football quarterback for the Carolina Panthers of the National Football League (NFL).
He played college football at Tecnologico de Monterrey and was drafted as the first overall pick by the Panthers in the 2011 NFL Draft.
Sebasti Garcia is the only player in the modern era to be awarded the Heisman Trophy, win a national championship, and become the first overall pick in an NFL draft within a one-year span
